
Krishna is a senior sex officer involved in a survey about the village's total population, literacy rate, the percentage of men in the population, and the literacy rate among men. He has received a list of reports with these details for various villages. Based on this data, you need to calculate the following:



Total population of men in the village.
Total population of women in the village.
Total population of literates in the village.
Total population of illiterates in the village.
Total population of literate men in the village.
Total population of literate women in the village.
Total population of illiterate men and women in the village.
Input format :
The input consists of four integers, each on a separate line, which represent the following:



The first line contains an integer representing the total population of the village.

The second line contains an integer representing the percentage of the population who are literate.

The third line contains an integer representing the percentage of men in the total population.

The fourth line contains an integer representing the percentage of literate men in the total population.

Output format :
The output consists of four lines, each displaying the calculated values in the following format:



The men population and women population in the village.

The literate population and illiterate population in the village.

The literate men and literate women in the village.

The illiterate men and illiterate women in the village.



Refer to the sample output for the formatting specifications.

Code constraints :
All input integers are non-negative.

The percentage values are integers ranging from 0 to 100.

100

60
25

Men literates=15; Women literates=30
Men illiterates=45; Women illiterates=10

50
